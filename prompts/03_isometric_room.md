# 模板 03 · 等距小屋剖面

把今天画成一个等距视角（isometric）的小屋/工作室剖面，房间里的每个角落正在发生今天的一件真实活动。像一个温暖的微缩世界。

## 适用

想要叙事感、能把多件事放进同一个空间的日子。具体性强——每个角落都是一件真事。

## Prompt 模板

```
A cozy isometric cutaway illustration of a small studio-home, vertical 3:4, viewed at a 45-degree isometric angle. Inside the rooms, different corners each show one real activity happening today — a tiny figure doing each:
{{ACTIVITIES}}  ← 把每件事放进一个房间角落（如 书房在写文章, 桌前在和AI结对编程, 厨房/客厅在陪孩子, 角落在冲咖啡, 小屏幕显示交易行情, 墙上贴着拍卖的酒）

Time of day: {{TIME}} — windows and lamp light should reflect morning/afternoon/evening.
Warm, charming, miniature-world feel, clean vector-ish shapes. Limited 6-color e-ink palette: black, warm red, golden yellow, blue, green on off-white. Each room corner must clearly depict a specific activity from today, recognizable at a glance. No text needed.
```
